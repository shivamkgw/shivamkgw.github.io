The DIGEST Project

€18k Seed Grant, Co-Lead. A spatiotemporal modelling framework to decode gut microbiome dynamics in early life and their downstream effects on the gut-brain axis.

MS Thesis · IISER TVM

Complex Networks & Time-Delayed Systems

Investigated synchronisation and stability in coupled oscillator networks under delayed feedback. Derived analytical bounds and validated them against large-scale simulations.
